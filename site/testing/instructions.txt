TESTING FOLDER DOCUMENTATION GUIDELINES
======================================

Folder: docs/testing/

Purpose:
This folder documents the testing strategy and practices used to ensure
correctness, stability, and reliability of the system.
It focuses on *what is tested, how it is tested, and why*.

Testing here supports:
- Confidence in business logic
- Safe refactoring
- Fair framework comparison

Writing Rules for This Folder:
- Tests must be deterministic and repeatable
- Avoid flaky test explanations
- Separate test intent from implementation details
- Emphasize correctness over coverage numbers
- Keep examples conceptual, not framework-specific

End of Testing Guidelines
